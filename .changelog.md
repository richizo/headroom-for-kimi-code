## [0.5.26] - 2026-04-15

### Added
- **proxy/moonshot:** transparent support for Kimi thinking mode. The `thinking` request parameter and `reasoning_content` response field are forwarded unchanged, and tests cover enabled, disabled, and instant modes.
- **proxy/moonshot:** streaming support for Moonshot `/v1/chat/completions`. `stream: true` requests are forwarded via SSE, preserving `delta.reasoning_content` and `stream_options.include_usage`.
